Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our store and services.

Do you offer delivery outside Eldoret?

Yes! We deliver across Kenya. Delivery within Eldoret town is free, while deliveries to other towns are charged based on distance and weight. Contact us for a delivery quote.

Do you offer instrument repair services?

Absolutely. Our experienced technicians handle repairs for all types of instruments including guitars, keyboards, drums, and audio equipment. Bring your instrument to our store for a free assessment.

Do you offer music lessons?

Yes, we offer professional music lessons for piano, guitar, drums, and vocals. Lessons are available for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced musicians. Contact us to schedule a session.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, cash, and major credit/debit cards. For premium instruments, we also offer flexible installment payment plans.

Can I return an instrument after purchase?

Yes, we offer a 7-day return policy for unused instruments in their original packaging. If there's a manufacturing defect, we'll repair or replace it under warranty.
